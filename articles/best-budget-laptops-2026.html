Best Budget Laptops Under 2500 RON in 2026

June 13, 2026 · By TechPicks Editorial

★★★★☆ 4.5 / 5

Finding a good laptop under 2500 RON is harder than it looks. The market is flooded with machines that cut corners on display quality, build, or battery — and you only discover this after unboxing. We tested 10 of the most popular options available on eMAG and Altex to save you the trouble.

Quick Verdict

For most people on a budget, a laptop with a modern AMD Ryzen 5 processor, 8GB RAM (upgradeable), and a 512GB SSD delivers the best value under 2500 RON. Avoid Intel Core i3 options in this price range — the AMD alternatives consistently outperform them in both speed and battery life.

What to Look for Under 2500 RON

Before diving into our picks, here's what matters most at this price point:

  • Processor: AMD Ryzen 5 7000-series or newer over Intel Core i3
  • RAM: 8GB minimum — check if it's upgradeable (soldered RAM is a dealbreaker for many)
  • Storage: 512GB SSD — anything less is too tight for Windows 11
  • Display: IPS panel, Full HD (1920×1080) — avoid TN panels
  • Battery: 45Wh+ for all-day use

Full Review

Budget laptops in Romania have improved dramatically over the last two years, largely thanks to AMD's Ryzen 7000-series mobile chips. What used to require a 4000+ RON budget — a fast, efficient processor with decent integrated graphics — is now available for under 2500 RON.

The display is where most budget laptops compromise, and our top pick is no exception: 250 nits is workable indoors but struggles in bright sunlight. If you work outdoors often, consider spending an extra 300–400 RON for a 300-nit panel. For most users working from home or in an office, it's perfectly adequate.

Battery life of 7 hours in real-world use (mixed browsing, documents, video calls) means you can get through a full workday without hunting for a plug. Fast charging brings it from 0 to 60% in around 45 minutes.
