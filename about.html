About TechPicks

TechPicks is an independent tech review site focused on helping you find the best value for your money. We test products hands-on — laptops, phones, headphones, and more — and share honest, unsponsored opinions.

How We Review

Every product we review is tested for at least one week under real-world conditions. We don't accept sponsored reviews or manufacturer-provided scores. If we recommend something, it's because we'd buy it with our own money.

Affiliate Disclosure

Some links on TechPicks are affiliate links, meaning we earn a small commission if you make a purchase — at no extra cost to you. This helps keep the site running and the reviews independent. We never recommend a product solely because of an affiliate arrangement.
